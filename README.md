### 1. [Amazon Sales Analysis](./Amazon%20Sales/)
**Objective:** Track key performance indicators (KPIs) against targets, analyze regional penetration, and identify top-performing products to optimize the sales strategy for 2025.

*   **Key Skills Demonstrated:** KPI tracking, Map Charts, Donut Charts, Funnel Charts, and complex data aggregation using Pivot Tables.
*   **Insights Discovered:**
    *   **Sales Performance:** Reached ₹ 1,02,26,625 in total sales, achieving 81% of the set target.
    *   **Volume & Reach:** Sold 33,874 units (92% of target) across 23 different cities (82% of target).
    *   **Top Products:** "Nestle Smarties Pop-Up" was the most popular product by volume with 5,267 units sold.
    *   **Geographic Trends:** Sales are heavily concentrated in Maharashtra and Delhi, with prominent peaks in city-wise performance.

### 2. [Road Accident Data Analysis](./Road%20Accidents/)
**Objective:** Analyze road accident datasets for 2021-2022 to identify patterns in casualties based on vehicle type, road conditions, and environmental factors to suggest improved safety measures.

*   **Key Skills Demonstrated:** Custom dashboard design, interactive timeline slicers, Donut Charts, and layered Pivot Tables.
*   **Insights Discovered:**
    *   **Total Impact:** Recorded 417,883 total casualties, with the vast majority being slight (84.1%), followed by serious (14.2%) and fatal (1.7%).
    *   **Vehicle Involvement:** Cars accounted for an overwhelming 79.8% of all casualties (333,485 incidents).
    *   **Environmental Factors:** The majority of casualties occurred during daylight (305k vs 112.9k in the dark) and on dry road surfaces (279k vs 115k on wet surfaces).
    *   **Geographic Distribution:** Urban areas saw significantly more casualties (255.9k) compared to rural areas (162.0k).
